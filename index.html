About

Rahee Group

Rahee group is a leading integrated railroad company based in India, operating in the field of Rail Tracks and Bridges for over sixty years. Established in 1948, the group specializes in Construction of Bridges, Track Installations, Flash Butt Welding of Rails, Turnout Systems, Fastening Systems, other Permanent Way Products and Bridge Products.

  • 1,500+ Team Members
  • 75+ Major Bridges
  • 200 K.M+ Track Constructed
  • 9+ Manufacturing Units

CORE AREAS OF

Businesses

Rahee undertakes turnkey Bridge construction projects involving super structure as well as substructure work. Super structure works that Rahee undertakes include fabrication, assembly and launching/erection of steel, PSC, RCC and composite super structure of all types. Sub structure works undertaken by Rahee includes Pile, Well and Open foundation.
